Every service at Seraphic Hair Studio begins with a one-on-one consultation. We take the time to understand your hair goals, assess your hair's condition, and recommend the best approach — so there are never any surprises, only results you'll love.

Our pricing reflects the quality of our craft and the premium products we use. Final pricing may vary based on hair length, thickness, and the complexity of the service. If you have any questions or would like guidance on which services are right for you, don't hesitate to reach out.
